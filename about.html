THE MD'S DESK
Canaaneast Group is a progressive company with diverse interests in multiple sectors. Our sound and secure investment portfolio strives to raise the bar in every sector we operate in by leveraging on our expertise and cutting edge technology to reimagine what’s possible. At Canaaneast Group, we live to exceed our customers’ expectations. To achieve this, we continuously benchmark our operations against world class standards to ensure our brand name is synonymous with top-notch quality. Our residential projects, for example, integrate modern architectural trends such as the innovative use of space and the optimal use of natural light, to redefine style and ambience. Our people are the backbone of our thriving enterprise. We are privileged to have professional and technical expertise and a vast network of more than 2,000 operating employees. Every day, we endeavour to make our
organisation an enjoyable place to work, where our employees can collaborate, innovate, grow and realise their potential. We have successfully positioned ourselves within the Kenyan market by establishing various subsidiaries engaging in different business spheres such as Tourism & Hospitality, Manufacturing, Agro and Food Processing, Construction & Real Estate as well as Interior Design. We are continously looking for new ways of adding value to the regions we serve and believe that the future holds immense opportunities for us to make a lasting impact.

THE DRIVE BEHIND OUR GROWTH

VISION

To be a leader in our chosen markets, respected for the quality of our products and services and for the way we conduct our business.

MISSION

  1. To be a world-class enterprise that is passionate about the standards of living in key growth markets in Africa.
  2. To deliver strong returns to our stakeholders by selling high-quality products and services at affordable prices, backed by excellent customer service.
  3. To lead the way in areas such as governance, sustainability and environmental conservation and set a good example for other companies to follow.

CODE OF CONDUCT

Earning, establishing and retaining trust and confidence inside and outside our business is vital to all activities within and related to the organisation. We maintain high ethical standards and are strong believers in doing the right thing in the right way. Individual companies within the Group adopt additional principles and processes to deal with specific ethical issues that affect them. We regularly monitor our performance and make improvements.

Our Code of Conduct guides and directs all our decisions and every action that affects our:

  • Communities
  • Competitors
  • Customers, Intermediaries and Suppliers
  • Employees
  • Environment
  • Government and Regulators
  • Shareholders and Investors
